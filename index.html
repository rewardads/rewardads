What is RewardADs?
RewardADs is a service that allow you to earn with ads in Minecraft!

How does it work?
It's very simple: you need to put RewardADs plugin on all your spigot servers; after this, you can connect the plugin to our database by gave code.
Make sure to configure the configuration.yml and rewards.yml files!
After did it, your site will host as soon on our site.
This operation will request to maximum few hours.
Once activated, you can earn from the comfort of your chair and RewardADs will take care of the rest!

How do payments works?
After everything is set up, the RewardADs team will get back to you asking your name on PayPal, to pay you.

When does RewardADs pay you?
Mostly, RewardADs' team will pay you the 25st of the month, but expect the payment between the 21st and the 26th of the month.

How much does RewardADs pay you?
Ads typically pay 0.002 per view and 0.30 per click,
since we have chosen to split the earnings 80% for you and 20% for RewardADs,
you will be paid approximately 25 cents per ad.


What service is used by RewardADs?
RewardADs, like most sites, relies on Google Ads service.
This means that all ads displayed on the RewardADs service, are powered by Google Ads.

Okay, but how I can add my Minecraft server?
You can contact us by sending a mail at helprewardsads@gmail.com to request your server!
